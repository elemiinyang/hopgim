About Us

Welcome to the House of Prayer and Global Intercession Ministry, where we are committed to transforming lives through the power of prayer and deliverance. Founded on the principles of faith, love, and service, our ministry is a haven for those seeking spiritual growth and breakthrough. Led by Senior Pastor John Okolo, our congregation is a family united in the pursuit of God's presence and purpose.

Mission Statement

We are committed to transforming lives, conquering kingdoms for Jesus through prevailing, intense and sustained prayer of intercession

Vision

Bringing healing and deliverance to nations through evangelistic, prophetic and apostolic intercession.

OUR FOCUS

The House of Prayer and Global Intercession Ministry places a strong emphasis on deliverance and intercessory prayer. We believe in the transformative power of prayer to change lives, heal the sick, and set the captives free. Our ministry is dedicated to standing in the gap for individuals, families, and communities, lifting their needs before the throne of grace. Through our various services and programs, we aim to equip believers to become powerful intercessors and witnesses of God's miraculous works.

Weekly Services

Saturday AM - Healing and Deliverance Service: Every Saturday morning, we gather for a powerful session of healing and deliverance. This service is designed to address spiritual, emotional, and physical ailments through prayer and the Word of God. Come and experience the transformative power of God's healing touch.

Monday - Hour of Personal Pentecostal Service: Join us every Monday evening for a deep and enriching personal Pentecostal service. This is a time for personal encounters with the Holy Spirit, worship, and prayer. It’s an opportunity for believers to receive fresh anointing, guidance, and strength for the week ahead.

Special Services

Youth and Single Night Vigil: On the last Thursday of each month, we hold a dedicated night vigil for youth and singles. This service is a vibrant and dynamic time of worship, prayer, and teaching, addressing the unique spiritual and practical needs of young people and singles. It’s a night of empowerment and encouragement, fostering a sense of community and purpose.

3-Day Family Deliverance Service: Every last quarter, we conduct a special 3-day family deliverance service. This intensive session is aimed at breaking generational curses, healing family wounds, and restoring familial relationships. Through targeted prayers and teachings, families can experience profound deliverance and unity.

Testimonies

Our ministry has been blessed with numerous testimonies of instant healing and deliverance. Many individuals have experienced miraculous recoveries and breakthroughs during our services. These powerful testimonies serve as a testament to the mighty works of God and inspire faith and hope in our congregation.


Community and Fellowship

We are more than a church; we are a family. Our ministry offers a range of programs and activities designed to foster fellowship, spiritual growth, and community engagement. From Bible study groups to outreach programs, we are committed to making a positive impact in Gwarinpa and beyond.
